About Leafletter

Leafletter helps track where leaflets have been distributed for promotion campaigns. Volunteers log their trips so others won't double-up; organizers can track progress and direct others.

Volunteers don't need accounts to log their leaflet drops, and their work is visible to all. Organizers need accounts to create and manage campaigns.

Colophon

Written by Sef Kloninger with Claude Code. Maps are provided by OpenStreetMap contributors, under the Open Database License.

The source code is available open and available on GitHub. Collaborators are welcome via pull request.

Event Management

Managing events is too hard to do from a phone. To create or update events, go to https://leafletter.app/manage from a computer.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Leafletter is a volunteer coordination tool for leaflet distribution campaigns. We are committed to being transparent about the limited data we collect.

Data we collect

  • Volunteers — No account is required. When you log a trip, the streets you select and a timestamp are stored. No name, email, or device identifier is associated with a trip.
  • Campaign organizers — Creating or managing a campaign requires an account. We store your username and email address.
  • Usage analytics — We use Google Analytics to understand aggregate usage patterns (page views, session counts). Google Analytics may set cookies and collect anonymized IP addresses. See Google's Privacy Policy for details.

What we do not collect

  • We do not use your device's GPS or location services.
  • We do not collect payment information.
  • We do not sell or share personal data with third parties.

Data retention and deletion

Organizer accounts and associated campaign data are retained while the account is active. To request deletion of your account or data, contact us at the address below.

Contact

Privacy questions or deletion requests: leafletter@kloninger.com.
